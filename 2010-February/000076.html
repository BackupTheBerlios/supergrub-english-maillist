I have noticed pointsec on your mail subject right now.

Here there is an article about it (I am not telling you to read it):
http://techrepublic.com.com/5208-6230-0.html?forumID=101&threadID=227767&start=0

USB startup disk creator must have installed grub into Windows hard 
disk. These involves usually not only modifying MBR but some sectors 
after the MBR and before the first partitions.

I bet that these sectors are used internally by pointsec and thus you 
have overwritten some information that pointsec needs to work.

I just hope that you are a good friend of your corporation IT staff 
because I think that a pointsec recovery is not easy.

Good luck! Do not forget to tell us what the IT staff does for solving 
this issue.
