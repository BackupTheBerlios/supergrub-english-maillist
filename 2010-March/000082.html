I didn't just want an archived copy of the original scripts, but I
wanted it to be easy for others to see what changes I made and why (so
that JustRon can incorporate some or all of them if he wants, and
others can point out a better approach). If you do a diff of JustRon's
bootiso.lua and listiso.lua vs the final isodetect.lua even with git
diff's --find-copies-harder it just shows up as a complete rewrite,
which it is not.
