Loadfile is still not implemented in grub's lua, it can be worked
around as you can see in baells_experimental but it seems like an ugly
hack to me (which is the main reason I haven't merged
baells_experimental into baells yet) and should be fixed in the next
release of grub (along with some other annoying lua related bugs).

But with loadfile support I would still have had a commit like this
because I think it's useful to have a record of each of the changes
made and why they were made rather than one large diff.
