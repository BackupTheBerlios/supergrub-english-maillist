    Only allow whitelisted characters in iso filenames
    
      * These filenames are passed as kernel parameters and interpreted by
      * various different initrd scripts depending on the distribution.
      * Since I can't be sure what characters would cause problems for
      * various distros (though whitespace seems a problem with almost all),
      * or how to escape these characters in a way each distro will
      * understand and safely handle, I've decided to use a fairly
      * conservative whitelist of charaters.
    
      * Current whitelist is alphanumeric characters, periods, underscores,
      * and dashes.
