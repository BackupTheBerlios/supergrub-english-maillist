Rescatux 0.30 beta 2 has improvements on UI:
* Now when you select an option it does not show other options but its 
descriptions.
* Now there is a BACK button
* There's no longer the name of the selected option plus Rescue button 
but a button that says: "Run option"

Enjoy!
