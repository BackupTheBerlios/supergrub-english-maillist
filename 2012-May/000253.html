If you want just a workaround to boot into your system whether is 
Windows or Gnu/Linux use Super Grub2 Disk.

If you want to recover your grub either you boot with Super Grub2 Disk 
and try grub-install /dev/sda (according to your device) from your system.

Or you can use Rescatux with its Restore Grub option.

Super Grub2 Disk is 2 MB size.
Rescatux is 460 MB size.
